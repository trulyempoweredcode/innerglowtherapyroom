Meet Josie

Hello, I'm Josie 💓

I created Inner Glow Therapy Room as a peaceful women-only space where clients can take time away from the stresses of everyday life and focus on relaxation, wellbeing and self-care.

With experience in beauty, massage and holistic treatments, my approach is centred around creating calming, personalised experiences that help clients feel relaxed, restored and cared for.

I believe treatments should feel more than just an appointment — they should be an opportunity to slow down, reset and leave feeling lighter both physically and mentally.

I specialise in holistic massage, luxury facials and relaxation-focused treatments using the beautiful MONU professional skincare range.

My aim is always to create a welcoming, calming environment where every client feels comfortable, relaxed and supported.
